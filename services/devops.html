DevOps is a combination of tools and practices that increases an organization’s ability to deliver products and services at a faster pace without compromising the quality of the software. The speed allows organizations to better compete in the market and enhance customer experience. It also allows businesses more time for innovating as the processes get streamlined and employee efficiency is improved.

Azure DevOps is a continuous delivery platform, orchestrator, and cloud provider from Microsoft that offers different DevOps tools for development and deployment of the software. AWS DevOps, on the other hand, is Amazon’s answer to implementing the DevOps philosophy using its cloud platform and dedicated tools and services.
