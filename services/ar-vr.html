Augmented Reality (AR) overlays digital content onto the real world, enhancing the user's environment, while Virtual Reality (VR) immerses users in a computer-generated environment. AR enhances the real world, while VR creates a new virtual world for users to explore.

AR/VR services have versatile applications across industries, including gaming, education, healthcare, real estate, marketing, manufacturing, and tourism. These technologies can improve user engagement, training, marketing campaigns, and streamline various processes.
