Our Process

Our process typically involves understand, define, sketch, decide, prototype and validate to ensure that the final product meets the needs of the user and delivers a positive experience.

01

Undertsand

When our peers overlook the pre-planning, we never fail to do the basics right. We ask the right questions to our stakeholders that centers around their target audience.

02

Define

By advancing the understanding of your goals and vision, we define the scope of work. We put years of experience and technical expertise to steer your project into the right direction.

03

Sketch

We sketch your business ideas into an epic. Epic is an integral part of the software development process, We map your business ideas.

04

Decide

This is the step where we involve our product developers, managers and finally, designers to decide on every data we’ve collected.

05

Prototype

Knowledge sharing and developing a shared understanding are our key priorities. The fourth phase is turning previously generated ideas, sketches and blueprints.

06

Validate

The final phase of the design sprint process is to validate the prototype with real and existing users to generate feedback. This phase validates your solutions before you kickstart developing an actual product
